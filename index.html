What is this app?

SquidNote is a Note app with built-in calendar and pack of Apple frameworks! It has a built-in, open source rich-text editor, tons of different types of making your note original and awesome!

Some of its features:

  • EXPORT AS A PAPER: take your note to the next level, print it on paper
  • Synchronize your work with iCloud across Apple devices
  • 100% Native. No multiplatform. Only fresh and new technologies by Apple
